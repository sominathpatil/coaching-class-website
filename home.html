About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Welldone Commerce Classes aims to provide high-quality education in commerce and professional courses, fostering academic excellence and career success. Our vision is to be a leading coaching institute recognized for innovative teaching, student-centric learning, and outstanding results. We strive to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in competitive exams while creating a nurturing environment that empowers them to achieve their academic and professional aspirations.

History of the Institute

Welldone Commerce Classes, established in 2001 in Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar (Aurangabad), specializes in commerce education, offering courses like 11th & 12th Commerce, CA Foundation, and CA Intermediate. There is no available information about NEET or JEE coaching at this institute. However, for NEET & JEE preparation, you may need to explore other specialized coaching centers in the area..

Achievements & Recognitions

Established in 2001, Welldone Commerce Classes has been delivering excellence in commerce education. The institute has produced outstanding results, including Aurangabad’s top HSC result, with Snehal Wani securing 96.33%. With consistently high success rates in CA Foundation and CA Intermediate exams, the institute has built a strong presence in Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar and Waluj Mahanagar.
